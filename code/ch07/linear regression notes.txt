一个点的横(x)坐标是这个点距离纵(y)轴的水平(横向)距离。

一个点的纵(y)坐标是这个点距离横(x)轴的竖直(纵向)距离。

yhat = b0 + b1 * x

(yhat-ybar)/sy = r   * (x-xbar) / sx)

y = yhat + residual (e: error)
y = b0 + b1 * x + e



assumptions:
1. e is not associated with x.  sometimes we call e the error term, which is not something we can control as x.
2. e is assumed to be a random noise.  (can be checked in the residual plot)
*3. no z
